More or Less

Welcome to the playroom!
The toys are all over the floor!
Let's help tidy up,
and put them back on the shelves.

we'll see which shelf has more toys,
and which has less !

to place them on the shelves.
All done!

Shelf A has 5 toys
Shelf B has 4 toys

and Shelf B has less toys!
When we have a lot,
it's More
When we have a little,
it's Less.
